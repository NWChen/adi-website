JADE

January Application Development Experience

Details

Sunday, Jan 10th —
Friday, Jan 15th

9am — 10pm daily

JADE is a week-long student-led, winter break program for first year students to learn how to build apps. Come experience all of the awesome tech resources in NYC by visiting tech companies like Facebook or AppNexus and small startups like Bradley Rothenberg. We will have a week-long crash course in web development, in which participants will be split into smaller groups to learn how to make web applications.

Meet other amazing Columbia students, work alongside upperclassmen mentors, and participate in a growing community of students who share an interest in programming and technology. Learn to take advantages of interesting classes and professor at Columbia and companies and events in the city. By the end of the web development bootcamp, participants will be equipped to build their own web applications.

This program will accept twelve first years excited to learn about programming. Prior programming experience such as an introductory Computer Science course (1002, 1004, 1006), AP Computer Science, or a similar experience will help you get the most out of this program.

Applications are due October 19th. If you have any questions about this program, please email jade@adicu.com!
